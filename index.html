How to add to Gmail:
  1. Fill in your details above — the preview updates live
  2. Click Copy Signature
  3. Open Gmail → Settings (gear icon) → See all settings
  4. Under General, scroll to Signature and click Create new
  5. Click inside the signature box and paste (Ctrl+V)
  6. Scroll down and click Save Changes
